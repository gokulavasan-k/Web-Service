United States


Location
The USA is a country in North America, bordered by the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, and neighboring Canada and Mexico.
Size
The USA is the world's third largest country by size and nearly the third largest by population.
Geography
The USA has a varied geography, with mountains in the west, a broad central plain, and low mountains in the east.
Government
The USA is a constitution-based federal republic with a strong democratic tradition. The president is the chief of state and head of government.
Capital
The capital of the USA is Washington, D.C.
Economy
The USA is often called an economic powerhouse, with the world's largest economy.
Major mountain ranges
The Rocky Mountains and Appalachian Mountains are major mountain ranges in the USA.
Major rivers
The Mississippi, Missouri, and Colorado are major rivers in the USA. The USA is also known as the United States (U.S.) or America.
